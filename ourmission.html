Our Mission

Woodie Flowers, the co-creator of FIRST®, has been a fountain of inspiration for members of the international FIRST® community. Gracious Professionalism® is a term coined by Woodie Flowers that describes the process of partaking in activities that encourage high-quality work, emphasize the value of others, and respect individuals and the community. The term Gracious Professionalism® shaped not only FIRST® Robotics Challenge and FIRST® as a whole, but every member and coach affiliated with the program.

It is time that Gracious Professionalism® was expanded beyond FIRST® because the lessons to be learned can be applied to every part of our lives. We invite you to help us honor his legacy of compassion and kindness by spreading experiences you’ve had with Gracious Professionalism® with #HowDoYouGP on your social media platforms. Our campaign aims to spread this message of positivity not just among FIRST® students, but to everyone around the globe.
